18 Card Game Rules

Welcome to 18, a strategic card game where you compete against the computer!

Game Setup

Both players start with two sets of cards numbered 1 through 9 (total of 18 cards each).

Gameplay

The game consists of 9 rounds. Each round has 2 phases:

  1. Phase 1: Both players secretly select a card to play. The cards are then revealed.
  2. Phase 2: Both players secretly select another card to play (can be any remaining card). The cards are revealed.

Winning a Round

After both phases, the player with the higher sum of their two played cards wins the round.

If the sums are equal, the round is a tie and no one gets a point.

Winning the Game

The first player to win 5 rounds wins the game. If no one reaches 5 wins after 9 rounds, the game is a tie.

Strategy Tips

  • Pay attention to which cards have been played
  • After Phase 1 is revealed, decide whether to chase with a higher card or conserve your high cards
  • Try to predict what cards your opponent might play based on their previous moves
