Game Rules

How to Play

  1. Each player starts with two sets of cards numbered 1-9 (18 cards total).
  2. The game consists of 9 rounds. First to win 5 rounds wins the game.
  3. Each round has two phases:
    • Phase 1: Both players play one card face down, then reveal
    • Phase 2: Both players play another card face down, then reveal
  4. After both phases, the player with the higher sum of both cards wins the round.
  5. If sums are equal, the round is a tie.
  6. The game ends when one player wins 5 rounds, or after all 9 rounds are completed.

Strategy Tips

  • Watch which cards your opponent has played
  • Balance using high cards to win rounds vs saving them for later
  • Try to predict your opponent's moves based on previous rounds
